We first create an indicator variable, Major Corporate Customer, which equals 1 if a supplier firm reports at least one corporate customer who

into account not only the number of major corporate customers j disclosed by a supplier firm i, but also the influence that each major corporate
customer j exercises on the supplier firm i’s revenues.25 The specific formula we use for Corp Customer HHI is as follows:
